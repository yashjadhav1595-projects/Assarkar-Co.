Setting up of an Ethics & Integrity Program


Training, overview, and drafting of the Ethics & Integrity Program.

Ethics and Integrity Program covers the Anti-corruption and Anti-bribery Policy, Gifts and Hospitality Policy, Conflict of Interest, Confidentiality, Protection of Intellactual Property Rights, whistle-blower Policies, etc., to demonstrate the values and culture of the organization and also to comply with the applicable laws. The Ethics and Integrity Program in place gives confidence to all the stakeholders i.e. clients, vendors, business associates, and all others associated with the business including its employees about the culture of transparency and fair dealing in the organization.
